Research

My current research focuses on quantum information theory and quantum foundations.

Research Interests

Quantum Randomness

Investigating the fundamental nature of randomness in quantum mechanics, including randomness certification, expansion, and amplification protocols.

Bell Inequalities & Nonlocality

Studying Bell inequalities, quantum nonlocality, and their applications in device-independent quantum information processing.

High-Dimensional Quantum Systems

Exploring quantum systems with high-dimensional Hilbert spaces, particularly those encoded in orbital angular momentum (OAM) of photons.

Quantum Steering

Investigating quantum steering phenomena and their applications in quantum information tasks, including randomness certification and entanglement verification.
